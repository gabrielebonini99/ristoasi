OUR STORY

A new style in restaurants

In 2001, Ristoasi was born, a group that, through franchising, wants to give more people the opportunity to share the fruits of the energy invested.

The favor met with customers is a sure guarantee of the effectiveness of our premises. High motivation, concrete solutions and clear objectives have allowed us to establish ourselves among the leaders in the catering sector in the regional area. The innovative formula, the careful management policy and the marketing strategy ensure the franchising an immediate success and broad development prospects in the years to come.

The customer as the center of our activity, the quality of the products and service, the care of the environments are the three rules that have guided our path, which is the result, first of all, of the deep passion that we put into our work.

The secret of the success of Ristoasi lies in the proposal: Mediterranean gastronomy, based on pasta and pizza, a guarantee of healthy and nutritious cuisine. The ingredients, always first choice.

The fast and accurate service, organized both for our "little" customers and for business breakfasts or dinners, can create that "family" feeling that is the sign of Made in Italy cuisine. Different environments in style and furnished with particular care, to satisfy every need of the customer and break the monotony. A recognizable menu and an ever-changing setting are characteristics of Ristoasi.
