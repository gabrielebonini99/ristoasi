PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: 12/07/2023

This privacy policy describes how Ristoasi SRL collects, uses and protects the personal information of its website users, as well as its customers and suppliers. Ristoasi SRL is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of the personal information of the individuals with whom it interacts. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our privacy practices.


Collection of personal information

Ristoasi SRL may collect personal information from various sources, including the website, registration forms, communications and contractual agreements. The personal information collected may include, but is not limited to: names and contact information (email address, telephone number and postal address), company information (company name, role and company address) and payment information (debit/credit card details and bank account).


Use of personal information

The personal information collected by Ristoasi SRL is used for the following purposes: providing the requested services (order processing, booking management and product delivery), communicating with users and providing customer service, maintaining a customer and supplier database, personalising the user's experience on the website, improving our products and services, sending promotional and marketing communications (subject to user consent).


Sharing your personal information

Ristoasi SRL may share your personal information with third parties only in the following circumstances: with service providers that work with Ristoasi SRL to provide the services you request (group premises, delivery companies), with regulatory or legal authorities if this is required by law or to protect our legal rights, with your consent.


Protection of your personal information

Ristoasi SRL uses technical, administrative and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted over the Internet.


Rights of data subjects

Individuals have the right to request the deletion of their personal information held by Ristoasi SRL. They can also object to the use of their personal information for marketing purposes. To exercise these rights, data subjects can contact us using the contact information provided below.


Cookies and similar technologies

The Ristoasi SRL website uses cookies and other similar technologies to collect information about users' preferences and improve the browsing experience. Please read our cookie declaration to learn more about how we manage cookies.


Privacy Policy Changes

Ristoasi SRL reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on the website and the effective date will be updated accordingly.


Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this privacy policy, please contact us by email at info@ristoasi.com, we will respond as soon as possible.
