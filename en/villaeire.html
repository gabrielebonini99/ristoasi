VILLA EIRE

Our history

Villa Eire was born in 2002 in an ancient historical home built in 1892. The place is attentive to the food and wine proposals of the Venetian tradition and lends itself perfectly to host banquets and ceremonies.

Where to find us

We are in via Giarola 3 in Sona, in the province of Verona.

Opening hours

We are open every day from 12.00 to 14.30 and from 18.30 to 00.30.
