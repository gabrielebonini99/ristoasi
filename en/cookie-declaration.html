COOKIE DECLARATION

Effective date: 12/07/2023

This cookie information explains how Ristoasi SRL uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on its website (ristoasi.com). Using the website, it is consenting to the use of cookies as described in this information. This information applies to both first-party cookies, set by Ristoasi SRL, and third-party cookies, set by third-party services.


What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are saved on your device (computer, tablet or mobile phone) when visiting a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly or to improve their efficiency, as well as to provide information to the owners of the website.


Cookie categories we use

We treat four different categories of cookies: those necessary, those of preferences, those of statistics and marketing.

The necessary cookies are essential for the correct functioning of the website. They allow basic features such as navigation of pages, the correct pop-up display, access to protected areas and e-commerce transactions. These cookies do not require the visitor's consent.

Preference cookies allow the website to remember the choices made, such as language preference or region, and offering improved and personalized features. These cookies require the visitor's consent.

Statistics cookies help us understand how users interact with the website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us to analyze and improve the performance of the website. These cookies require the visitor's consent.

Marketing cookies are used to trace users between different websites in order to show relevant and engaging announcements. These cookies can collect personal information and require the visitor's consent.


Third-party cookies

We also use third-party cookies on our website for various purposes, including analysis, advertising and integration of social media. These cookies are set and controlled by third-party service providers and are subject to their respective privacy policies. It is recommended to consult the privacy policies of these third-party suppliers to understand how information is collected and used.


Cookies management

Technical cookies are considered necessary cookies and therefore it is not possible to avoid the installation on the visitor's device. Users can instead choose whether to accept or refuse optional cookies, in any case it will be possible to continue normally with navigation within our website. Optional cookies consent can be easily modified or revoked by taking advantage of the widget placed at the bottom left. However, it remains possible to remove or deactivate cookies through the settings of your browser, but the deactivation of cookies could significantly limit some functionality of the website.


Changes to the Cookie Declaration

Ristoasi SRL reserves the right to update or modify this information on cookies, also called the Cookie Declaration, at any time. The changes will be published on the website and the date of entry into force will be updated accordingly.
