LA TONDA

Our history

The traditional cuisine goes married with innovative ideas and with the classic tasty pizza, which represents this historic place of the Ristoasi group. We have been operational since 1994.

Where to find us

We are in via Brennero 10 in Pescantina, in the province of Verona.

Opening hours

We are open every day from 12.00 to 15.00 and from 18.30 to 00.30.
